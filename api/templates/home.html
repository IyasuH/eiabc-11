Ethiopian Institute of Architecture, Building Construction and City Development

History

EiABC, the Ethiopian Institute of Architecture, Building Construction and City Development was founded in 1954 as the “Ethio-Swedish Building College” and later merged with the Addis Ababa University. EiABC was reformed in 2010 as an autonomous Institute of Technology under the umbrella of Addis Ababa University to foster the development, capacity and competitiveness of industries in the field of Architecture, Design, Construction, Urbanism, Environmental Planning, Management and Technology. EiABC provides study programs at Bachelor, Master and PhD levels; EiABC conducts research and offers consultancies in all fields concerning the built environment. EiABC addresses a wide range of topics, such as private sector support, international standards and quality management, and professional education at university level.

About Us

This website was created to be a collection of resources and project files for students in the departments of Construction Technology and Management, Architecture and Urban Building. The files provided have been generously contributed by senior students and alumni of the university who wish to help their fellow students succeed.

The aim of this website is to support students during their studies by providing access to materials like lecture notes, course assignments, project guidelines and sample files from previous years. The files are organized by department and course so students can easily find resources related to their specific classes and areas of study. All files are shared freely under a Creative Commons license to enable open learning and collaboration within our university community.

We hope that the materials provided here will help supplement students' formal learning and make their coursework and projects a little bit easier. The contributions of our senior students and alumni are invaluable in passing down knowledge, tips and resources that only come with experience. If you wish to contribute your own course files to help future students, please contact us through the form provided on the 'Contribute' page. Together we can build a truly valuable resource for all students in our university's specially focused on construction, architecture and building departments.
