CoTM

The undergraduate program of Construction Technology and Management is designed to prepare students for careers in the construction industry. The program combines coursework in construction technology, project management, and business to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the field. Students learn about construction materials, methods, and technologies, as well as project planning, scheduling, and budgeting. They also develop skills in communication, leadership, and teamwork, which are essential for success in the construction industry. Graduates of the program are well-equipped to pursue careers in construction management, project management, estimating, and other related fields. They are also prepared to continue their education in graduate programs in construction management or related fields.
